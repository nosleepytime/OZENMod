Why OZENMod

A moderator that reasons, not a blacklist

Blacklists punish the innocent and miss the clever. OZENMod normalizes evasion tricks, scores context locally, and asks an AI only when a message is genuinely ambiguous.

Context-aware AI

Conversation history, targets, irony and intent — ambiguous messages get real judgement, with a verdict and a confidence score.

Local-first speed

Spam, flood, links, evasion and obvious toxicity are decided on your machine in under 5 ms. The AI is the exception, not the rule.

Explainable decisions

Allow, delete, warn, timeout, ban or human review — every action is logged with a reason you and your mods can read.

Fair warning ladder

Strikes before sanctions, escalating timeouts if you prefer, exemptions for mods and VIPs — and instant action on severe content.

Evasion-proof filters

Leetspeak, homoglyphs, zero-width characters, s p a c i n g — normalization folds the tricks before rules run.

Your keys, your control

Pollinations free tier by default. Plug OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini or a local Ollama — keys never leave your computer.

How it works

Protected in three steps

1Download & connect

Install the app (Windows/macOS) and link Twitch with a device code — your account or a dedicated bot account.

2Start the bot before you stream

Open OZENMod, press Start — or let it auto-start when your stream goes live. Everything runs on your computer.

3Stream worry-free

Watch decisions land in the live feed with reasons. Tune rules from the app or the web dashboard, even mid-stream.

FAQ

Questions, answered

Is it really 100% free?

Yes. The bot runs on your computer, the dashboard runs on free hosting, data lives on Firebase's free plan and the default AI (Pollinations) needs no account. There is no paid feature today — a future premium tier will only add optional cloud hosting.

Do you store my chat?

No. Messages are analyzed in memory on your machine and never persisted. Warnings and session stats are temporary and deleted automatically when your stream ends.

What if the AI gets it wrong?

Uncertain cases go to a human-review queue instead of a sanction, every action is explained and reversible, and “mark as false positive” tunes your channel's thresholds.

Does it replace my human mods?

It works with them. Mods and VIPs are exempt by default, the review queue is built for them, and the dashboard gives them the same visibility you have.
